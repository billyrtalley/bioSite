Adam's motocycles include a Harley Davidson, Yamaha FZ-09, and a 1975 Honda CB200T. Adam is in the process of giving his 2019 Toyota RAV-4 a high-tech upgrade. True to his love of tech, Adam has resurrected an older Roland electronic drum set in favor of a classic set of drums. Another added benefit of his drums, Adam’s son and daughter are wild about dad’s new hobby.
